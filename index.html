About the Alps

The highest and longest mountain range among the systems lying entirely in Europe. At the same time, the Caucasian mountains are higher, and the Ural mountains are longer, but they also lie on the territory of Asia. The Alps are a complex system of ridges and massifs, stretching outwardly from the Ligurian Sea to the Mid-Danubian lowlands
